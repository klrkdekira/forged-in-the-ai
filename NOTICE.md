## Content policy

Per <https://bladesinthedark.com/licensing>, the following must never be
committed to or distributed with this project:

- The Duskwall / Doskvol setting, its districts, maps, and named NPCs
- The assembled core-book playbooks and crew sheets: playbook name paired
  with starting action dots, ability selections, starting builds,
  friends/rivals lists, per-playbook items, and crew claim maps/upgrades.
  This includes the free sheet PDFs from bladesinthedark.com/downloads;
  free to download is not licensed to redistribute.
- Artwork from the core book
- "Blades in the Dark" in the name of this product

The SRD itself, including its special-ability bank, item rules, and tables,
is CC-BY and may be committed as content-pack data with the attribution above.

Owners of the game may load their own copies of core-book content locally as
private content packs, including uploading rulebooks they own to be parsed
into private modules. Uploaded books and everything derived from them remain
local user data and are never committed, exported, or redistributed. The
repository ships only SRD-derived mechanics and original example content.
